What are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files stored on your device when you visit a website. They contain information that allows us to remember your preferences and improve your browsing experience.

At Catalyst HR System, we use cookies responsibly to provide you with the best possible experience while respecting your privacy.

Did you know...?

Cookies cannot access other files on your computer or contain viruses. They are simply text files that help enhance your web experience.

Types of Cookies

Managing Your Cookies

You have complete control over the cookies in your browser:

Google Chrome

Settings → Privacy and security → Cookies

Mozilla Firefox

Options → Privacy & Security

Safari

Preferences → Privacy

Microsoft Edge

Settings → Privacy, search, and services

Important

If you disable all cookies, some platform features may not work correctly. Essential cookies are necessary for basic functionality.
